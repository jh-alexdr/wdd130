History

Jhalex Deep Rafting Water was founded in 2012 by two adventurous engineers, Jhon T. and Alexander T., inspired by their love for innovation and white-water rafting. The company's mission was to revolutionize the rafting experience by combining cutting-edge technology with eco-conscious practices..

Jhalex quickly became a leader in the rafting industry, offering both custom rafts and organized rafting tours. Their innovation not only redefined adventure tourism but also promoted sustainability by using recycled materials and funding water conservation efforts.
